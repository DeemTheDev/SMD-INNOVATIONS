Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services does SMD INNOVATION Technologies?

    SMD INNOVATION Technologies specializes in Azure migration services, Azure implementations, and Microsoft 365 services. We help businesses migrate their applications, infrastructure, and data to the cloud and provide end-to-end solutions for setting up and managing Microsoft 365 applications, such as email, SharePoint, and Teams.

  • What is the process for Azure migration?

    Our Azure migration process involves several key steps: a. Discovery and assessment: We analyze your current infrastructure, applications, and business requirements. b. Planning: We create a comprehensive migration plan tailored to your specific needs. c. Migration: We execute the migration process, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations. d. Testing and validation: We test and validate the migrated applications and infrastructure to ensure optimal performance and functionality. e. Optimization and management: We provide ongoing support and optimization to ensure you get the most out of your Azure environment.

  • How long does an Azure migration take?

    The duration of an Azure migration depends on various factors, such as the complexity of your existing infrastructure, the number of applications and data to be migrated, and your specific requirements. After our initial consultation and assessment, we will provide you with a detailed timeline and migration plan.

  • How much does an Azure migration cost?

    The cost of an Azure migration depends on several factors, including the size and complexity of your existing infrastructure, the number of applications and data to be migrated, and any additional services you may require. To provide you with an accurate quote, we encourage you to contact us for a personalized consultation

  • Will there be any downtime during the migration process?

    Our team strives to minimize downtime during the migration process. We use proven techniques and best practices to ensure a seamless and secure transition to the Azure cloud. However, some migrations may require a brief period of downtime, which will be carefully planned and communicated to minimize disruption to your operations

  • How secure is my data during the migration process?

    At SMD INNOVATION Technologies, we take data security seriously. We adhere to industry-standard security practices and compliance requirements to safeguard your data during the migration process. We also ensure that your Azure environment is configured to protect your data and applications once the migration is complete.

  • Can you help with Microsoft 365 setup and integration?

    Yes, we offer end-to-end Microsoft 365 services, including email migration, SharePoint setup, and Teams integration. Our experts can help you deploy, configure, and manage Microsoft 365 applications, enabling seamless collaboration and productivity across your organization.

  • Do you provide ongoing support after migration?

    Absolutely! At SMD INNOVATION Technologies, we believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients. We offer ongoing support, optimization, and management services to ensure that your Azure environment and Microsoft 365 applications continue to perform at their best, driving growth and success for your business.

  • How can I get started with SMD INNOVATION Technologies?

    To get started, simply contact us through our website or reach out via email or phone. Our team will schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and how we can help you with your Azure migration or Microsoft 365 services.

  • Are your Azure migration experts certified?

    Yes, our team of Azure migration experts hold various Microsoft certifications, including Azure Administrator and Azure Solutions Architect ensuring that you receive the highest quality service and support throughout your migration journey.
